Don't wait for the right pick

You choose what can work for you, don't wait for the right one coming up to you.
If you don't have already the skill,whatever it is, take time to learn it and then just try it.
Give yourself 2 weeks to learn the skill and then create a plan on how to monetize it, once you have done it, give yourself one month to show it to the world.
Just try and let me know, ok?

Personal experience

What I told you above is what I've done, nothing more. At the start of June this year, I began searching for something that could fit me.
After having tried almost nothing, I decided to learn by watching YouTube videos of 5+ hours on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which, to be honest, I didn't fully understand.
I watched these videos for two and a half weeks and tried to implement what was being taught.
I won’t hide the fact that I only understood HTML and CSS, but this didn't stop me from trying to create a basic website.
I started with this post, and although I was 'helped' by the other two members of my team, whom you can get to know better on the About page,
I was the one most determined to create this website. In fact, every page, except that one, was made by me alone.

The creation of the website

I know it isn't the best-looking website, but I focused most of my efforts on the content.
I worked on it from June 17th to July 20th, creating one page per day. While working on new pages, I also edited the old ones.
The week before publication was the hardest because I had to create the Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions pages.
These needed to be customized with the website URL, which was quite stressful.
I don't know if this website will bring me any money; the plan was to share my journey and potentially earn a small percentage from affiliate commissions.
It might not work, but if it does, you'll see everything with full transparency on my Instagram*.

*overall_small

How does it work

Full transparency: I have applied for Amazon Associates, and if you buy the books mentioned in this post,
I will earn a commission from the sale without any extra cost to you. I've only applied for this program.
If I earn anything additional, I'll inform you either on Instagram or through another post on the site.
I want to succeed and prove that you can too by following my journey.
I won’t hide anything because if I can make it with full transparency, no one can make videos like 'Luigi Longobardo exposed' or anything like that.
I don't want you to be arrogant or greedy; you might succeed while I might not, even if you followed me, but that's part of the journey.
I'm not saying you have to tell everyone everything; there's nothing wrong with keeping some things to yourself.
But if you want to share something, just do it.

Last things

There are people you just hate and don’t want to reveal anything to, and that’s completely fine.
My family tells me, 'Don't say anything to anyone.' In my opinion, people don’t care unless you've already succeeded.
I’ve shared my plan with some people, but they didn’t care; I was just talking to people who weren’t interested.
Normal people can’t understand our point of view, sitting in a sports car with our spouse, both dressed up
—they call us dreamers and say, 'You don’t know anything about real life, get a job.'
I just hate those people. I tell them how simple life can be now, but all they do is complain.
So if you want to share your opinion on this topic with like-minded people, just do it because others don’t see what God showed you.
It’s understandable not to take advice from a beginner, but if you’re facing issues during your journey, mine also involves helping you.
