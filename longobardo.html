My first time

Not really the first time you may think, anyway I was turning 15 year old and I had my heart broken in a very bad way and started to hit the gym.
I wasted the most precious time to get the most of the newbie gains and in fact I have still an average physique*,
I started dieting at the same time and it was the only thing I was doing perfectly.
I was so motivated and inspired by fitness influencers that I barely skipped workouts.
If you want to know, I'm part of the ones who wanted a 6-pack and almost never trained them,
because being the same size of a fridge in lean muscle mass is definitely better.

* You can check yourself on ig:overall_small

How I dealt with daily fapping

Yes, I'm still ashamed of myself. Days spent with my phone in my other hand, just for temporary pleasure.
Very disgusting you may think, and you're right, but it's finally over.
I've been almost 2 years without touching myself and I fucked up everything on May 14th.
But this was the last time I promise, I won't fall in temptation because God is with me and even if He is disappointed, I'm not going to leave Him ever again.

Routines

I've already told you something on the other post on routines, anyway I don't follow any particular routine.
I just started setting daily goals this summer, I write them on a small notebook on the same day or the day before and I still don't clear all of them.
I hate doing nothing, but sometimes I get up from the chair where I'm working or learning and lay on the bed and watch the roof for even hours!

Business

I have an idea it's similar to a plan, I don't know if it can work for me, but if you can see this website it means that it can work.
I'm not going to give details here, but I's like a chain of causes and effects, it's all linked.
I don't think it's going to be easy to achieve a decent wealth with this plan,
because much more people are better than me and since I've seen them doing it, I think luck will do a big part if I succeed.
Either way I'm going to build something that can work for me and can make me successful,
because I'm not going to stay in my current position for the rest of my life, I must reach the top 1%.

Where Do I Aim to be?

I'm going to reach the top 1%, I want to meet the people I admire and spend time with them,
I want to drive luxury cars, I want beatiful homes everywhere, I don't want to work for anyone else except myself.
I want to achieve greatness, my family doesn't share my goals, but I'm going to show up with a beatiful wife, in an expensive suit,
driving cars that have a value of so much they can't even imagine.
I don't call these dreams, I call them goals, dreams are for kids and I'm a man with goals, that's why I'm going to achieve every single one of them,
no matter what.

Books I've read

I've read a few of the books of the other post and I've tried to explain what I've read in the most simple way I knew, for the others I admit it I've asked ChatGPT, I haven't bought them yet but they're on the shopping list.
I've read Rich Dad Poor Dad, Atomic Habits, Think and Grow Rich, How to Win Friends and Influence People,
Can't Hurt Me (italian version), How to Win the Game of Life and I'm currently reading The Psychology of Money.
I've learnt a lot, I hope I can put what I've learned in practice as soon as possible.
Unfortunately there were some concepts I didn't fully understood but I'm going to read them again to understand them better.

Last things

I've written everything I minded to show you how I'm going to live and where I personally started.
There's nothing wrong with sharing your goals, especially if they match with the goals of someone else who's like minded.
Don't worry about the ones that don't understand, because I'm with you, I'm a Mr Nobody right now,
but I'll just say 'Start, whatever does make sense to you and succeed'.
Write them anywhere, or just think about them daily, put in the work, find what you want and a way on how to achieve it.
Burnout doesn't exist, it's just your mind quitting like when you're training hard.
